The Akinlade Family

This is a brief introduction about how the Akinlade Family was started.

  • 2017- Toba Arrived the United States from Nigeria and met Lori at the church he attended where they became friends.
  • 2018- Lori and Toba started dating at the beginning of the year.
  • 2019- They kept being committed to the relationship and Toba knew he was going to marry Lori.
  • 2020- Toba proposed to Lori and they got married the same year.
  • 2021- Lori and Toba welcomed their first child Ella-Marie Fisayo.
  • 2022- The trio continued to live happily together with expectations of an addition to the family the following year.
  • 2023- Tayo Scott was born and the Akinlades became a family of four.
  • 2024- The family of four have continued to live happily ever after.
